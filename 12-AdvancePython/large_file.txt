The current President of India is Droupadi Murmu. She is the 15th president to hold the office and assumed charge on July 25, 2022. 
Key Facts About President Droupadi Murmu
First Tribal President: She is the first person from India's designated tribal communities to be elected President.
Second Female President: Murmu is the second woman to hold the office after Pratibha Patil.
Youngest President: At 64 years old when she took office, she is the youngest person to assume the post and the first President born in an independent India.
Background: Before her presidency, she served as the Governor of Jharkhand from 2015 to 2021 and had a career as a teacher and legislator in Odisha. 
Role of the President
The President of India is the head of state, the first citizen of the country, and the Supreme Commander of the Indian Armed Forces. The President's primary duty is to preserve, protect, and defend the Constitution and the law. All executive actions are formally taken in the President's name, though the powers are generally exercised on the advice of the Prime Minister and the Council of Ministers.
